ABOUT $LAIKA

Laika Token is a meme coin inspired by Laika, the brave Soviet space dog who made history in 1957 as the first living being to orbit Earth aboard Sputnik 2. Though her journey ended in tragedy, Laika’s story became a symbol of exploration, sacrifice, and the quest for the unknown.

Laika Token channels that spirit, blending meme culture with a tribute to space history. Built as a community-driven project, it aims to raise awareness around both technological advancement and animal welfare. More than just a meme, Laika Token is a cosmic homage to a true pioneer — reminding us that even the smallest among us can reach for the stars.
